GRS provides premium service at a public sector price, with unique access to best-in-class sifting and selection tools, and exclusive access to innovative pre-employment screening.

GRS offers bespoke selection and assessment services for a specific and diverse candidate market, with cutting edge end-to-end recruitment data and insight. Our highly skilled workforce have an in-depth understanding of Civil Service regulations

GRS offers its users the following functions and services:

  • Keyword search enables candidates to search for job vacancies using keyword matching
  • Job alert frequency can be adjusted to allow candidates to specify the number of job alerts they recieve
  • Welsh language use throughout allows candidates to search and apply in Welsh
  • Job advert gender debiasing allows vacancy holders to automatically debias a job advert to ensure gender neutrality
  • Job advert layout changes provide an improved user experience
  • When candidates close their accounts, the automated candidate withdrawal function withdraws any live applications
  • Improved accessibility adheres to the new WCAG 2.2 Standards
  • Candidate account change tracking ensures that changes are logged to enable improved query handling
