The GRS team receives and responds to a large number of emails. These emails include queries and requests for help about many different subjects and processes associated with GRS.

This is a varied, complex and time-consuming method of communication, as GRS team members have to receive emails, check information and answer queries within a limited time frame.

To improve the query process, we have designed a new automated system that gives users the ability to ask questions, get guidance and engage with GRS without having to send emails.

With direct access to different types of query and a simple, easy to use process, we are sure that users will be able to access information, guidance and to interact with GRS more efficiently and faster than ever before.
