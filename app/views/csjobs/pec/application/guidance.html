The application is split into 2 sections.

Section 1 - Basic information

We will ask you to provide basic information such as your:

  • name and contact details
  • eligibility for the job
  • nationality

We will also ask you diversity and inclusion monitoring questions.

Section 2 - Further details and supporting evidence

When you have completed section 1, we will move your application to the next stage, section 2, where you will be asked to give us further information, which will be assessed.

Before you start

Consider the following points:

  • Civil Service Jobs will timeout after 45 minutes of inactivity
  • You cannot make changes to a section once it has been submitted
  • You cannot resubmit your application if you withdraw it
  • Multiple applications for the same job may result in all of them being withdrawn
  • We will email you about your application as it progresses
