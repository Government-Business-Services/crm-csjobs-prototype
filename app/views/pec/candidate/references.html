Checking your activity

We need the details of 3 people who can confirm what you have been doing during the last 3 years.

An employer or academic referee must:

  • be from within the last 3 years
  • not be related to you by birth or marriage

Any other referee must:

  • have known you for more than 3 years
  • not be related to you by birth or marriage
