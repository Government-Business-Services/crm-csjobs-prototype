The recruitment process for bringing candidates into the Civil Service begins with a vacancy advertisement. As a vacancy holder, there’s a lot to consider.

Starting with getting the approval for a vacancy and then its advertisement, through creating an effective and eye-catching advert that will attract candidates, to the final onboarding of successful applicants, you’ll need to manage a lot of details.

On this page you can access information to help you manage the process and publish effective advertisements that are accurate and useful. There’s guidance about creating, managing and editing advertisements, and to make things smoother you’re able to use self-serve forms to make requests for actions.
