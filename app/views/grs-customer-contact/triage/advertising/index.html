Recruiting candidates into the Civil Service begins with a vacancy advertisement. The recruitment process proceeds from creating a vacancy advertisement through to onboarding successful applicants.

On this page you can access information about creating, managing and editing advertisements. You will also find some self-serve forms to make requests for actions, and there is guidance about how to make advertisements accurate, useful and effective.

Guidance

Advertising a vacancy

Use the options below to get information about creating a vacancy, managing advertisements and to access information about vacancy campaigns.

Advertisement approvals

To get advertisement approvals you should follow the procedures set out in your department’s guidance.

Amend a vacancy advertisement

Use this online form to request an amendment to an active advertisement that is live online.

Manual recruitment request

Use this online form to make a manual recruitment request for a vacancy.

Vacancy approvals

To get approval for a vacancy you should follow the procedures set out in your department’s guidance.

Vacancy campaign planning

Find out more about how to plan and start a recruitment campaign for a vacancy.

Vacancy supporting documents

You may need to provide certain documents in support of the created vacancy. You can access a list of documents and check the ones you need.

Vacancy request form

Use this online form to make a request for a vacancy.

Withdraw a vacancy advertisement

You can fill in an online form to request the withdrawal of a vacancy’s advertisement.
